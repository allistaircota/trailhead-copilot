# Trailhead Supply Co. — Shipping Policy

_Last updated: January 2026_

All orders ship from our warehouse in **Denver, Colorado**. We currently ship to
addresses within the **continental United States**.

## Order Processing

Orders are processed within **1–2 business days** of being placed. Orders placed on
weekends or holidays begin processing the next business day. You'll receive a shipping
confirmation email with tracking as soon as your order leaves our warehouse.

## Shipping Methods & Costs

| Method | Delivery Estimate | Cost |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Standard** | 5–7 business days | $6.99 |
| **Expedited** | 2–3 business days | $14.99 |
| **Overnight** | Next business day | $29.99 |

Delivery estimates are counted **after** processing and reflect business days only.

## Free Shipping

Orders with a subtotal of **$75 or more** qualify for **free Standard shipping**.
**Trail Club members receive free Standard shipping on every order**, with no minimum.

## Tracking Your Order

Every shipment includes a tracking number from our carrier partners (**UPS, FedEx, or
USPS**). Track your package from the link in your shipping confirmation email or from
**Order History** in your account.

Shipment status moves through these stages:

- **Processing** — order received, being packed
- **Shipped** — handed to the carrier, tracking is active
- **In Transit** — moving through the carrier network
- **Out for Delivery** — on the truck for delivery today
- **Delivered** — arrived at your address
- **Delayed** — the carrier has reported an exception (weather, address issue, etc.)

## Delayed or Lost Packages

If tracking shows no movement for more than **5 business days**, or a package is marked
delivered but you haven't received it, contact **support@trailheadsupply.example**. We
will open a carrier investigation and, where appropriate, reship or refund your order.

## Address Changes

We can only change a shipping address while an order is still in **Processing**. Once a
package has shipped, the address is locked by the carrier.
