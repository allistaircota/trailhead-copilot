# Trailhead Supply Co. — The Trailhead Guarantee (Warranty Policy)

_Last updated: January 2026_

We stand behind the gear we sell. The **Trailhead Guarantee** covers manufacturing
defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

## Coverage Periods

- **Standard coverage: 1 year** from the delivery date on most products.
- **Extended coverage: 2 years** on **tents and backpacks**, which take the most abuse
  on the trail.

Coverage length for a specific item is listed on its product page and in your order
confirmation.

## What's Covered

- Seams, zippers, buckles, and stitching that fail under normal use
- Fabric or material defects (delamination, coating failure)
- Hardware defects (broken frames, poles, or fasteners)

If we confirm a manufacturing defect, we will **repair, replace, or refund** the item at
our discretion, at no cost to you.

## What's Not Covered

- Normal wear and tear from regular use
- Damage from accidents, misuse, improper storage, or unauthorized repairs
- Cosmetic wear that does not affect function
- Products marked **Final Sale** are still covered for manufacturing defects but are not
  eligible for return under our standard Return Policy

## Warranty vs. Returns

The Trailhead Guarantee is **separate from our 30-day Return Policy**. Returns are for
items you simply don't want to keep and have a time limit. The warranty covers
**defects** and applies for the full coverage period — there is no 30-day cutoff for a
genuine manufacturing defect.

## Filing a Warranty Claim

1. Email **support@trailheadsupply.example** with your order number and photos of the
   issue.
2. Our team reviews claims within **3 business days**.
3. If approved, we send a prepaid shipping label (when we need the item back) and
   process your repair, replacement, or refund.

Warranty claims require **proof of purchase** — your order number is enough.
