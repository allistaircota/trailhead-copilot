# Trailhead Supply Co. — Return & Refund Policy

_Last updated: January 2026_

At Trailhead Supply Co., we want you to get outside with gear you trust. If something
isn't right, we're here to help.

## The 30-Day Return Window

You may return most items within **30 days of delivery** for a full refund, as long as
the item is **unused, in its original packaging, and has all tags attached**.

- The 30-day window is measured from the delivery date on file, not the order date.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within **5–7 business days** after
  we receive and inspect the item.
- Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  (wrong or defective item shipped).

## Footwear

Boots and shoes may be returned within the same **30-day window**, but they must be
**unworn outdoors**. Trying footwear on indoors is fine — that's the only way to check
the fit. Items with visible outdoor wear (dirt, scuffs, trail damage) cannot be
returned.

## Final Sale & Clearance Items

Items marked **Final Sale** (typically clearance and heavily discounted products) are
**not returnable or refundable**. A product's Final Sale status is shown on its product
page and in your order confirmation.

## Trail Club Members

Members of our free **Trail Club** loyalty program enjoy an **extended 60-day return
window** on eligible (non–Final Sale) items. Sign in to your account before checkout to
receive the benefit.

## Defective or Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged or develops a defect, it is covered by the **Trailhead
Guarantee** rather than this return policy. See our Warranty Policy for details — there
is no time-limited return window for manufacturing defects.

## How to Start a Return

1. Sign in and open **Order History**, or contact support with your order number.
2. Select the item and reason for return to generate a prepaid return label.
3. Drop the package at any carrier location within 7 days of receiving the label.

Questions? Email **support@trailheadsupply.example** (Mon–Fri, 8am–6pm Mountain Time)
or use live chat on our website.
