# Trailhead Supply Co. — Frequently Asked Questions

_Last updated: January 2026_

## Orders & Tracking

**How do I track my order?**
Use the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email, or open **Order History** in
your account. Tracking becomes active once your order status changes from Processing to
Shipped.

**Can I change or cancel my order?**
You can cancel or edit an order while it is still in **Processing** (usually the first
1–2 business days). Once it ships, we can't change it — but you can use our 30-day
return policy.

**My tracking hasn't updated in several days. What should I do?**
If there's no movement for more than 5 business days, contact
**support@trailheadsupply.example** and we'll open a carrier investigation.

## Payments

**What payment methods do you accept?**
All major credit and debit cards, plus Apple Pay and Google Pay at checkout.

**When am I charged?**
Your card is authorized at checkout and charged when the order ships.

## Sizing & Products

**How do I choose the right size?**
Each product page includes a sizing chart. For footwear and apparel, we recommend
measuring and comparing to the chart rather than relying on your usual size.

**Do you price match?**
Yes. If you find a lower advertised price on the same item at an authorized retailer
within **14 days** of your purchase, contact us and we'll refund the difference.

## Trail Club Membership

**What is Trail Club?**
Trail Club is our **free** loyalty program. Members get free Standard shipping on every
order, an extended 60-day return window, and early access to seasonal sales.

**How do I join?**
Create an account and opt in — there's no fee. Make sure you're signed in at checkout to
receive member benefits.

## Returns & Warranty

**What's your return window?**
30 days from delivery for unused items (60 days for Trail Club members). Final Sale
items are not returnable. See our Return Policy for details.

**Something I bought is defective. What now?**
Manufacturing defects are covered by the Trailhead Guarantee for 1 year (2 years on
tents and backpacks) — see our Warranty Policy. This is separate from the return window.
