# Trailhead Supply Co. — Customer Support Playbook (Internal)

_Last updated: January 2026 · For support agents and the AI copilot_

This playbook describes how to handle common customer situations consistently. When
answering a customer, ground every policy statement in the linked policy document and,
when a question is about a specific order, look up the order record before responding.

## General Principles

- **Be warm, clear, and specific.** Confirm the customer's order number when relevant.
- **Never guess about policy.** If a detail isn't in a policy document, say you'll check
  rather than inventing an answer.
- **Escalate** billing disputes, safety issues, and anything outside policy to a human
  supervisor at escalations@trailheadsupply.example.

## Scenario: "Where is my order?"

1. Look up the order by order number and read its **status** and **shipment** record.
2. If **Processing**: reassure the customer it ships within 1–2 business days.
3. If **Shipped / In Transit**: share the carrier, tracking number, and estimated
   delivery date.
4. If **Delayed**: apologize, explain the carrier exception, and offer to open an
   investigation if there's been no movement for 5+ business days.
5. If **Delivered** but not received: open a carrier investigation and offer reship or
   refund per the Shipping Policy.

## Scenario: "I want to return an item"

1. Confirm the item is within the **30-day window** (60 days for Trail Club members).
2. Confirm the item is **unused with tags** (footwear must be unworn outdoors).
3. If the item is **Final Sale**, explain it is not returnable, but check whether the
   issue is actually a **defect** (which the warranty covers instead).
4. Generate a prepaid return label and explain the 5–7 business day refund timeline.

## Scenario: "My item is defective / damaged"

1. Route to the **Trailhead Guarantee**, not the return window — defects have no 30-day
   cutoff.
2. Confirm coverage period: **1 year** standard, **2 years** for tents and backpacks.
3. Request the order number and photos; claims are reviewed within 3 business days.
4. If approved, offer repair, replacement, or refund.

## Scenario: "Can you price match?"

- Yes, within **14 days** of purchase against an authorized retailer's advertised price.
  Refund the difference to the original payment method.

## Scenario: "How do I care for my gear?"

- Point the customer to the relevant section of the **Gear Care Guide**. Remind them
  that damage from improper care isn't covered by the warranty.

## When to Use Which Source

- **Policy questions** (returns, shipping, warranty, membership, care) → answer from the
  policy documents.
- **Account-specific questions** (this order, this shipment, stock levels) → look up the
  business records first, then answer.
